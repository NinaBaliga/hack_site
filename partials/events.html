Up Coming Events
NoVA National Day of Civic Hacking!
Saturday May 31. 8am to 9pm.
At National Science Foundation.
Come Hack with Us! RSVP
Details:
The National Science Foundation and Code for America's Northern Virginia Brigade are teaming up again to host our second National Day of Civic Hacking with a pre-event social hour on Friday night, May 30.
Come join other civic engaged residents and technology enthusiasts - from industry, non-profit, government and education sectors - as we create exciting new civic-based applications.
You'll have the exciting opportunity to work with national data sets from the National Science Foundation and local datasets and applications from the City of Alexandria and Arlington County.
Prizes:
You can also win one of a kind prizes from the NSF, and other cool techie toys from our sponsors.
Directions:
4201 N Wilson Blvd, Arlington, VA
From the atrium, go down the hall marked "North Elevators". We'll be in the conference rooms behind the guard station.
